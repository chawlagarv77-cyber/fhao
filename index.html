About Us

Garv Chawla School is a premier K–12 institution located in New Delhi, India. We focus on holistic development through academics, sports, arts, and technology. Our students are future-ready — guided by experienced faculty and a progressive curriculum aligned with NEP 2020.

Our motto: “Learning Beyond Books.”

Academics

We offer a modern curriculum from Pre-Primary to Grade XII, integrating core subjects with digital literacy, critical thinking, and creativity. Our timetable includes six dynamic periods daily with a balanced focus on sciences, arts, and personal development.

  • Smart classrooms
  • STEM & Robotics Labs
  • Weekly innovation projects

Sports & Co-Curriculars

Sports are at the heart of Garv Chawla School. Students participate in cricket, football, basketball, swimming, athletics, and yoga. Our annual “Garv Championship” celebrates team spirit and discipline.

We also offer music, art, dance, and debate clubs to nurture creativity and leadership.

Campus & Facilities

Our 10-acre campus includes:

  • Modern digital classrooms
  • Library with 15,000+ volumes
  • Indoor and outdoor sports arenas
  • Science & Robotics laboratories
  • Health & wellness center
